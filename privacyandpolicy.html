Privacy Policy - NoTrace Browser

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NoTrace Browser, a privacy-focused web browser developed by TriVisionTech.

Your privacy is our core principle. NoTrace is designed to ensure that your browsing activity remains completely private and under your control.

2. Core Privacy Principles

  • No user accounts required
  • No tracking or profiling
  • No data collection
  • No data sharing with third parties

3. Data Collection

NoTrace Browser does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or browsing data.

We do not collect:

  • Browsing history
  • Search queries
  • IP addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or telemetry data

4. Local Data Storage

Any data generated during your browsing session (such as cache, cookies, or history) is stored locally on your device only.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • No remote servers are used
  • You can clear all data at any time

5. Private Browsing

NoTrace Browser may offer private browsing features designed to minimize local data storage.

When enabled, browsing data is automatically deleted after the session ends.

6. Permissions

The browser may request standard permissions (such as internet access) required for basic functionality.

No permissions are used for tracking or data collection purposes.

7. Third-Party Content

Websites you visit may include third-party content (such as embedded videos, ads, or scripts).

These external services operate independently and may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

8. Security

NoTrace Browser is designed with privacy and security in mind, but users are responsible for:

  • Keeping their device secure
  • Avoiding malicious websites
  • Maintaining updated software

9. Data Retention

NoTrace Browser does not retain any user data externally.

All local data remains on your device until you delete it manually or uninstall the application.

10. Children's Privacy

NoTrace Browser does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, including children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
